About Me

Mission Statement

Welcome to Quartz Queen Yoga! My mission is to provide a comfortable environment for all students. Yoga can be a personal journey and rather cringe-worthy if you're nervous about attending your first class or even being sweaty around others. No worries here, let me guide you through your practice peppered with lame jokes. At QQY I encourage sangha (community) and learning. I make time and space for students to ask questions about yoga and connect with other students in a personable environment.

The goal of QQY is for everyone to become at peace with your body as I guide you through each Hatha yoga practice, whether you’re a first-time yogi or a Yoga Master. By the end of practice, you’ll be ready to hop up, leaving all your insecurities on the mat.

Class Offerings

Quartz Queen Yoga offers semi-private and private hatha yoga classes, giving individualized attention and instruction while delivering a customized level of yoga teaching.

Quartz’ Align & Flow Class crafted with the beginner in mind while accommodating intermediate and experienced students. Focuses on the staple yoga postures, breath and teaching proper alignment. Props and modifications are offered for an easeful class.

Magnetic Meditation There is energy all around us, take a moment to align with that energy using meditation. Class consists of a beginning breath work then progressing into guided mindfulness meditation.

Lava Strength Class designed to get your muscles moving and the sweat dripping. This class moves through the more intermediate to advanced yoga postures at a faster pace.
